# Global Superstore Dashboard

## Objective

The objective of this project is to design an interactive Power BI dashboard that provides insights into sales performance, profitability, customer behavior, product performance, and regional trends. The dashboard enables business stakeholders to make data-driven decisions through interactive visualizations, KPI monitoring, and time-series analysis.

## Dataset

**Dataset Used:** Global Superstore Dataset

The dataset contains information related to:

* Sales and Profit
* Orders and Customers
* Product Categories and Sub-Categories
* Customer Segments
* Regions, Markets, Countries, and Cities
* Shipping Information
* Order Dates and Time-Based Data

## Tools Used

* Power BI Desktop
* Google Slides
* CSV Dataset (Global Superstore)

## Project Features

* Interactive KPI Cards for Sales, Profit, Orders, Customers, and Profit Margin
* Time-Series Analysis using Monthly Sales and Profit Trends
* Product Performance Analysis by Category, Sub-Category, and Product
* Customer Segmentation and Customer Sales Analysis
* Regional Sales and Profit Analysis
* Interactive Slicers and Filters
* Multi-Page Dashboard Navigation
* Consistent Business Dashboard Theme

## Key Insights

* Technology generated the highest sales among all product categories.
* Copiers emerged as the most profitable sub-category.
* The Consumer segment contributed the largest share of total sales.
* The United States generated the highest overall profit.
* Sales and profit trends revealed seasonal fluctuations throughout the year.
* A small group of customers contributed significantly to overall revenue.

## Outcome

The project successfully transformed raw business data into an interactive dashboard that provides meaningful insights into sales, profit, customer behavior, and regional performance. The dashboard helps stakeholders monitor business performance, identify growth opportunities, and support strategic decision-making through data visualization and analytics.
